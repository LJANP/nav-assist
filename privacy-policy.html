Privacy Policy — NavAssist

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Data Collection

NavAssist does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. All data processed by the extension stays entirely within your browser.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

  • Export field preferences (which columns to include when exporting)
  • Message templates (subject and message text you write)
  • Temporarily captured prospect data during an active export session

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

External Communication

NavAssist makes zero network requests. No data is sent to any external server, API, analytics service, or third party.

Permissions

  • Storage: Used to save your preferences and templates locally.
  • Host permission (linkedin.com): Required for the extension to read prospect information from LinkedIn pages and display its UI elements.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
